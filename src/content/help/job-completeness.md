Every job can carry a completeness mark from 0 to 100% so anyone opening the job can see how far along the work is — and who said so.

## Mark it

1. Open the job's detail view (click the job anywhere it's linked — Stages, the dashboard, schedule).
2. Under the status pipeline, find the **Completeness** card.
3. Drag the slider to the right percentage and press {{button:green|Mark}}.

The card shows the current value, a progress bar, and who marked it last with the date:

:::example after marking
**Completeness — 80%** with "Marked by Roberto · Jul 13" underneath.
:::

You can move the mark down as well as up — corrections are fine, because every change is recorded.

## Who marked it, and the history

Each mark writes a {{chip:green|Progress}} entry in the job's activity feed (the notes panel below), stamped with the person and time, e.g. *Completeness: 60% → 80%*. Use the **Status** filter in the activity bar to see the full trail of marks alongside status changes.
